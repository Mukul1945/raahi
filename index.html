Simple steps to do Arabic bride. if you are naturally fair, a good bronzer can be used to get the perfect Arabic complexion. You should use foundation before any other makeup if your skin tone is uneven or if blemishes are visible as for any makeup your skin complexion needs to be flawless. You can use a concealer to hide any fine lines. In Arabic style makeup, as the eyes are focused and given the most importance, you should use an eyelid primer. This primer allows the eye shadow to be applied smoothly that lasts for long time. While doing Arabic makeup for your lips choose and apply lip color of deep shades like plum, red or brown.
